In preparation for your appointment, here are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Eat a light meal before your appointment as it may be difficult to eat after the procedure.
  • Wear comfortable clothing and avoid wearing any jewelry or accessories that may interfere with the dental equipment.
  • Discuss any anxiety or concerns you may have with your dentist beforehand so that they can take steps to make you feel more comfortable during the procedure.

If you need to reschedule your appointment or have any questions, please do not hesitate to contact our office.
